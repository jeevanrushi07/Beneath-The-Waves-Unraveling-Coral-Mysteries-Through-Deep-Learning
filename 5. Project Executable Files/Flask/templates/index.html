About

In the dynamic realm of Beneath the wave exploration technology, achieving accurate image classification is an essential element for coral research, mission success, and the advancement of scientific understanding.

Data Augmentation

In the data augmentation phase, diverse and augmented datasets are generated by applying transformations such as rotation, scaling, and flipping to the original coral images. This process enhances the model's ability to generalize by exposing it to a broader range of variations in the training data.

Model Building

In the process of building a space image classification model, attention to architecture design, hyperparameter tuning, and optimization techniques is crucial. Ensuring a well-structured model and leveraging advanced algorithms contribute to accurate and efficient classification of coral images.

Predicting

Upon completing the model building phase, predicting with the coral image classification model involves inputting unseen data and obtaining output predictions. Leveraging the trained model's knowledge, predictions are generated, providing valuable insights into the nature and features of new coral images.
